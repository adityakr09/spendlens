## FAQ — 5 real Q&As

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**
A: Completely free, no credit card, no login required to see your results. SpendLens is a free tool built by Credex — a company that sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. If your audit shows significant savings, we may reach out about whether Credex credits could help further. We only ask for your email after you've seen your results, and you can share your audit without ever giving us your email.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in the audit engine is pulled directly from official vendor pricing pages and verified with a date stamp. Pricing changes frequently — we update PRICING_DATA.md weekly. If you spot an error, email us and we'll fix it within 24 hours.

**Q: Does this work for large enterprise plans?**
A: Yes, but with a caveat. Enterprise plans are often negotiated and vary by company. The audit uses published list prices as a baseline. For companies with custom contracts, the plan-level recommendations still apply (e.g., "you have 50 Enterprise seats but only 15 active users"), but the exact dollar savings will differ from your negotiated rate.

**Q: Why do you ask for team size and use case?**
A: The right AI tool plan depends on how you use it, not just what you pay. A 2-person team on Claude Team is overpaying (there's a 5-seat minimum). A 20-person engineering team using Copilot for coding might save money switching to Cursor. Team size and use case let the audit engine give you relevant recommendations instead of generic ones.

**Q: What does Credex do?**
A: Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — from companies that overforecast or pivoted away from AI tools. The discounts are real and substantial. For teams with significant AI spend, Credex can cut costs on top of what plan changes alone achieve. [Learn more at credex.rocks](https://credex.rocks).
