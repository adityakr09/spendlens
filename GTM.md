# GTM

## Exact target user

**Engineering Manager at a Series A SaaS startup, 8–25 engineers, US or UK.**

Not "founders." Not "startups." Specifically: the person who approves the AI tool line items on the company credit card, gets a quarterly budget review from the CFO, and has personally felt the awkwardness of justifying a $4,000/month AI tools bill without a clear breakdown. They're technical enough to evaluate tools but busy enough that they haven't audited their stack in 6+ months. They use Slack, follow Hacker News, and are on X/LinkedIn mostly for professional content.

Job titles: Engineering Manager, VP Engineering, Head of Engineering, CTO (at companies <40 people).

## What they Google / scroll through right before they'd want this tool

- "cursor vs github copilot 2026"
- "how much should we spend on AI tools per developer"
- Hacker News: "Ask HN: What's your team's AI tool stack?"
- Their company's Notion finance tracker when CFO asks for budget cuts
- The monthly Stripe invoice from Anthropic that just hit $800
- LinkedIn posts about "AI tool consolidation" after a round of layoffs in their network

## How to get first 100 users in 30 days, $0 budget

**Week 1: Hacker News "Show HN"**
Post on a Tuesday or Wednesday at 8am ET. Title: "Show HN: I built a free tool that audits your AI tool spend and finds plan mismatches." Include one concrete finding in the post body (e.g., "Most teams on Claude Team with <5 seats are paying for unused seats they don't need"). Respond to every comment within the first 4 hours. Target: 30 signups from this alone if it hits front page.

**Week 1–2: Targeted Reddit posts**
Post to r/ExperiencedDevs: "I audited AI tool spend across 20 startup stacks — here's what most teams get wrong." Include the 3 most common findings as content (not a sales pitch). Link to SpendLens at the end as the tool behind the research. Target: 15–25 users.

**Week 2: Cold DM to X/LinkedIn**
Search X for "Cursor + team" or "GitHub Copilot + budget" in the last 7 days. DM 20 engineering managers who tweeted about AI tool costs. Message: "I saw your tweet about [specific thing]. I built a free 2-minute audit for this — no email required to see results. Would it be useful?" Target: 10–15 users from 20 DMs (50% conversion is realistic when the message is specific and the tool is genuinely free).

**Week 3: Lenny's Slack**
Find the #tools-and-resources channel. Post a message about the most surprising finding from running audits ("We've found that 60% of teams using GitHub Copilot Enterprise with <10 seats are paying for features they never use"). Link the tool. Target: 20–30 users.

**Week 4: Product Hunt launch**
Schedule a Product Hunt launch. Get 5 upvote commitments from connections first. Target: 50+ upvotes, 20 users.
